#### Flaubert on having kids
[A quote by Gustave Flaubert](https://www.goodreads.com/quotes/607288-the-idea-of-bringing-someone-into-the-world-fills-me)

_The aggravations and the disgrace of existence_ 🤌🏽

> “The idea of bringing someone into the world fills me with horror. I would curse myself if I were a father. A son of mine! Oh no, no, no! May my entire flesh perish and may I transmit to no one the aggravations and the disgrace of existence.”

I found this on an insta post of somebody making their way through Madame Bovary and I was immediately struck by this observation.
